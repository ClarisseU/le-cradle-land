WELCOME TO LE CRADLE LAND

WHAT IS LE CRADLE LAND

Le Cradle Land is the other home for kids, when mom and dad are at work looking for a living; it is a place with guarantee of safety for the kids so that the parents may go and work without worrying that something bad will or can happen to their little angel.
