WELCOME TO LE CRADLE LAND

Le Cradle Land was given this name to express what parents and children and Rwanda in general need. It is a home for kids as parents are out there making a living for them, a Rwanda's other family. A place where children are loved, cherished, natured into their true selves, it if a place for them to relax, enjoy and grow their thoughts in security.


WHAT IS LE CRADLE LAND

Le Cradle Land is the other home for kids, when mom and dad are at work looking for a living; it is a place with guarantee of safety for the kids so that the parents may go and work without worrying that something bad will or can happen to their little angel.

ABOUT US
